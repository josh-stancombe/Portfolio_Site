Store Application Coding Challenge
Project Specification: In our quest to stay in touch with what's going on in the commercial world we've decided to open a supermarket - we sell only three products:

  • Code: FR1 | Name: Fruit tea | Price: £3.11
  • Code: SR1 | Name: Strawberries | Price: £5.00
  • Code: CF1 | Name: Coffee | Price: £11.23

Our CEO is a big fan of buy-one-get-one-free offers and of fruit tea. She wants us to add a rule to do this. The COO, though, likes low prices and wants people buying strawberries to get a price discount for bulk purchases. If you buy 3 or more strawberries, the price should drop to £4.50 each. Our checkout can scan items in any order, and because the CEO and COO change their minds often, it needs to be flexible regarding our pricing rules.

Your goal is to implement a checkout so that it will scan items in and calculate total prices correctly for any combination of the products and offers above.
